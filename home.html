Gold

Gold stocks are shares in gold which an individual owns in a gold company, either a mining corporation, gold Mutual Funds or Exchange Traded Funds.

Nifty 50

The NIFTY 50 is a benchmark Indian stock market index that represents the weighted average of 50 of the largest Indian companies listed on the National Stock Exchange.

Nifty 500

NIFTY 500 is India’s first broad-based stock market index of the Indian stock market. It contains top 500 listed companies on the NSE.
